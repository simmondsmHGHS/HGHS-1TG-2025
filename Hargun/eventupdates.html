Event Updates

Sew It, Show It - On the 16th of August
Join our lunchtime sewing session in the Textiles Room! Whether you're a pro with the machine or just learning, come stitch something cool. Materials will be provided, just bring your imagination.

What's Your Style? - Wall Launch on the 23rd of August
We're launching a display wall in the hallway outside the A9 featuring student outfits and mini style quotes. Submit a pic and a short quote about your fashion vibe to be featured!

Custom Shirt Design Challenge - Entries Due 30 August
Design a fashion club shirt! The winning design will be printed and worn by the committee. Submit your digital sketch or hand-drawn design to win a free shirt with your design too!
