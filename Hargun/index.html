HGHS Fashion Club

Welcome to the Hamilton Girls High School Fashion Club!
This is where creativity meets style. From fashion tips and DIY ideas to event updates and student style inspo, we are all about expressing who we are through what we wear.

Everyone is unique look deserves to be seen, shared, and celebrated.
Whether you are a fashion expert or just love putting together cool outfits, theres a place for you here. Check out our Outfit of the Day below - updated daily! Click the arrow to see the past looks and keep up with our style streak.

Each day we feature one stunning look submitted by HGHS students. Want to be featured? Contact us by email on hka23239@my.hghs.school.nz or meet us in A9 every second week at lunch on Thursday.
