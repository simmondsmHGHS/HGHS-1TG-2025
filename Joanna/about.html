Information about the Photography Club

This club was created for students who are interested in photography.

The aim of this club is to create a community for people who share a common interest in photography to allow them to pursue their passion.

This club is open to new members and allows any student to join no matter their skill level.

How this club runs:

Students are given a camera to take photos with for two weeks.

Once the two weeks are up, all members of the club meet in D5 at lunchtime to look over all of the photos accumulated over the two week timeframe.

All members decide on the best photos taken which will then be posted to the schoolâ€™s social media (HGHS Facebook and Instagram).
