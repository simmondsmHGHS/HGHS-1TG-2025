Suya

History on Suya. Suya is delicious grilled, smokey and roasted goat meat dipped and seasoned with spice. In addition to this delicacy, some vegetables like onion and fruits like cucumber and tomatoes make this delicacy delicious and healthy. This dish originated from the hausa people in Nigeria. This delicacy can be eaten on its own or eaten with pap or garri (cassava put in cold water with sometimes groundnut)
