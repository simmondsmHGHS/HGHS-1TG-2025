INFORMATION

The HGHS Robotics club is a club where people who are interested in robotics can come together as a community.

In the Robotics club, you can learn how to create robot programs and are able to participate in challenges and competitions like Robocup.

This club is held at lunchtime on Tuesdays in A5 for anyone who may be interested!
